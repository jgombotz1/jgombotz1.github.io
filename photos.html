Hiking to Lake 22 in Washington State

This is one of my closest friends from home, Erik. He is such a positive and adventurous I thought this photo captured his personnality perfectly.

I captured this photo on a hike overlooking Cal Poly just after it had rained for a few days in Slo. It's amazing to watch the hills transform from brown to green here on the Central Coast, it seems to happen in a matter of hours after it rains.

This image was taken as I was walking along the stream that runs through downtown San Luis Obispo. The wind was still, the water was calm, and I couldn't help but stop to admire and capture the reflections created by the surrounding trees.

I took this picture in Mount Vernan, Washington, where every Spring 100's of acres of land transform into a sea of tulips. It's beauty never fails to amaze me.

Just another beautiful day at Pismo Beach. It's hard not to take living in San Luis Obispo for granted. Every season is full of sunshine, surfers and smiling faces. No wonder it's been called the happiest place in America.

I stumbled upon this park as I was driving along the coast towards Morro Bay. I never expected to find such a beautiful spot, it is still one of my favorite places to spend time in San Luis Obispo.

I'm always working to improve my star photography, but I think this is one of the best night shots I've captured so far. It was taken on January 1st, 2017 in Leavenworth, Washington. The green glow in the background was not visible to the naked eye, and it is still a mystery as to where it came from in this photo. It makes me appreciate it even more.
